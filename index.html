Welcome to Ryde Road Physiotherapy

At Ryde Road Physiotherapy, we believe in detailed assessment and treatment specific to the individual and their goals. We have a “hands-on”, exercise prescription and self-management approach to our treatment. Our practice is located within Hunters Hill Medical Practice. As such we are able to work efficiently and effectively with the GP’s and network quickly with Allied Health professionals to provide the optimal treatment for our patients. Our mission is to work in unison with our patients to ensure they receive the highest care possible.

Ryde Road Physiotherapy is located on 6 Ryde Road, Hunters Hill. We are open 6 days per week providing services to the general public.

We have the Isoft system (similar to HICCAPS) which is an electronic health claiming and payment system allowing you to process and finalise your claim through your private health insurer automatically after your treatment.

We welcome Department of Veteran’s Affairs, Workers Compensation and CTP (Motor vehicle accidents) cases.

Our Services

At Ryde Physiotherapy we provide the following services:

  • Strapping/taping
  • Rehabilitation post orthopaedic surgery
  • Women’s health
  • Veteran’s affairs patient
  • Clinical Pilates classes
  • Running technique and programs
  • Hydrotherapy programs prescriptions
  • Chest physiotherapy
  • Home visits
  • Worker Cover NSW treatment providers
  • Dry Needling and Western Acupuncture
  • Pre-sport screening for sports participation (amateur to elite)
  • Balance training / falls prevention exercise programs
  • Ergonomic assessment
  • Gait analysis
  • Biomechanical assessment
  • Work Conditioning programs
